Maneesha does Chekov tonight

THE THREE SISTERS ACT 3 author Anton Chekov

ANFISA

[Drinks water]

I'll refuse the post. I can't... I'm not strong enough....

OLGA

[Excited]

Forgive me, Olga, forgive me... I didn't want to annoy you.

ANFISA

[Kisses her.]

Remember, dear... we have been brought up, in an unusual way, perhaps, but I can't bear this. Such behaviour has a bad effect on me, I get ill... I simply lose heart!

OLGA

I've grown ten years older to-night.

ANFISA

[Enter]

Where's Masha? It's time we went home. The fire seems to be going down.

[Stretches himself]

OLGA

What is it?

ANFISA

[Retires with NATASHA to the back of the room. CHEBUTIKIN enters; apparently sober, he stops, looks round, then goes to the wash-stand and begins to wash his hands.]

He hadn't touched a drop for two years, and now he suddenly goes and gets drunk....

[Fire-alarm behind the stage.]
